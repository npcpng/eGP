## For Suppliers

### Supplier Registration

1. **Visit the eGP Portal**
   - Go to https://egp.npc.gov.pg
   - Click "Register as Supplier"

2. **Complete Registration Form**
   - Company Name
   - Trading Name (if different)
   - Business Registration Number
   - Tax Identification Number
   - Contact Details (email, phone)
   - Business Address

3. **Upload Required Documents**
   - Business Registration Certificate
   - Tax Compliance Certificate
   - Company Profile
   - Bank Account Details

4. **Verify Email**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account

5. **Complete Profile**
   - Log in to your account
   - Complete your company profile
   - Select your business categories (UNSPSC codes)

### Subscription Plans

To bid on government tenders, you need an active subscription:

| Plan | Annual Fee | Max Active Bids | Tender Value Limit |
|------|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Basic** | K 2,500 | 5 | Up to K 500,000 |
| **Standard** | K 5,000 | 15 | Up to K 2,000,000 |
| **Premium** | K 10,000 | 50 | Unlimited |
| **Enterprise** | K 25,000 | Unlimited | Unlimited |

**How to Subscribe:**

1. Go to "My Subscription" in the sidebar
2. Click "Subscribe Now"
3. Select your preferred plan
4. Choose payment method:
   - Bank Transfer (BSP)
   - Mobile Money (Digicel CellMoni / bmobile)
5. Complete payment
6. Upload proof of payment
7. Wait for NPC verification (1-2 business days)

### Finding Tenders

1. **Browse Open Tenders**
   - Go to "Bidder Portal" in the sidebar
   - Use filters to find relevant tenders:
     - Category
     - Procurement Method
     - Value Range
     - Deadline

2. **Tender Details**
   - Click on any tender to view:
     - Full description
     - Technical specifications
     - Evaluation criteria
     - Required documents
     - Q&A/Clarifications

3. **Save Favorites**
   - Click the bookmark icon to save tenders for later

4. **Set Up Alerts**
   - Enable email notifications for new tenders in your categories

### Submitting Bids

1. **Prepare Documents**
   - Technical Proposal (PDF)
   - Financial Proposal (PDF/Excel)
   - Company Profile
   - Required certifications

2. **Start Bid Submission**
   - Click "Bid Now" on the tender page
   - Accept terms and conditions

3. **Upload Documents**
   - Technical Proposal section
   - Financial Proposal section
   - Supporting documents

4. **Enter Bid Amount**
   - Enter your total bid price
   - Specify validity period

5. **Review & Submit**
   - Review all information
   - Confirm submission
   - Your bid will be encrypted and sealed

6. **Confirmation**
   - You'll receive a bid reference number
   - Email confirmation sent
   - Bid remains sealed until opening time

**Important Notes:**
- Bids cannot be modified after submission
- Late bids will be rejected automatically
- Keep your bid reference number safe

### Managing Contracts

After winning a contract:

1. **Contract Signing**
   - You'll be notified of the award
   - Download contract documents
   - Sign and upload signed copies

2. **Track Milestones**
   - View all contract milestones
   - Submit deliverables for each milestone
   - Track payment status

3. **Submit Invoices**
   - Upload invoices per milestone
   - Attach supporting documents
   - Track payment status

## For Government Agencies

### Creating Tenders

1. **Access Tender Management**
   - Go to "Tenders" > "Create Tender"

2. **Basic Information**
   - Enter tender title
   - Select procurement method
   - Choose category
   - Set estimated value
   - Set submission deadline

3. **Technical Specifications**
   - Add detailed requirements
   - Upload specification documents

4. **Evaluation Criteria**
   - Define evaluation method
   - Add scoring criteria
   - Set weightings

5. **Submit for Approval**
   - Send for internal approval
   - Wait for approval workflow

6. **Publish**
   - Once approved, publish to portal
   - Suppliers will be notified

### Evaluating Bids

1. **After Deadline**
   - Access "Evaluations" page
   - Select tender to evaluate

2. **Bid Opening**
   - Conduct bid opening ceremony
   - Decrypt and open all bids
   - Generate opening report

3. **Preliminary Examination**
   - Check bid responsiveness
   - Verify required documents
   - Mark non-responsive bids

4. **Technical Evaluation**
   - Score each criteria
   - Add comments/justification
   - Calculate technical scores

5. **Financial Evaluation**
   - Review financial proposals
   - Apply evaluation formula
   - Calculate combined scores

6. **Generate Report**
   - Create evaluation report
   - Submit for approval

### Awarding Contracts

1. **Create Award Recommendation**
   - Select winning bidder
   - Enter award justification

2. **Approval Process**
   - Submit for approval
   - Track approval status

3. **Notify Bidders**
   - System sends notifications
   - Unsuccessful bidders informed

4. **Contract Creation**
   - Generate contract from template
   - Add terms and conditions
   - Send for signing

## For NPC Officers

### Subscription Verification

1. **Access Admin Panel**
   - Go to "Admin" > "Subscription Payments"

2. **Review Pending Payments**
   - View list of pending verifications
   - Check payment details:
     - Supplier name
     - Plan selected
     - Amount paid
     - Payment method
     - Transaction reference

3. **Verify Payment**
   - Cross-check with bank records
   - Verify transaction reference
   - Review proof of payment

4. **Approve or Reject**
   - Click "Verify" to activate subscription
   - Click "Reject" if payment invalid
   - Add notes for rejected payments

5. **Supplier Notification**
   - System automatically notifies supplier
   - Email sent with subscription details

### System Administration

1. **User Management**
   - Add/edit/deactivate users
   - Assign roles and permissions
   - Reset user passwords

2. **Organization Management**
   - Register government agencies
   - Set agency hierarchies
   - Assign agency officers

3. **Workflow Configuration**
   - Configure approval workflows
   - Set value thresholds
   - Define escalation rules

4. **AI Governance**
   - Enable/disable AI modules
   - Configure thresholds
   - Review AI audit logs

5. **System Reports**
   - Generate compliance reports
   - Export procurement data
   - Review audit trails

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does subscription verification take?**
A: Typically 1-2 business days after payment submission.

**Q: Can I modify my bid after submission?**
A: No, bids are encrypted and sealed. You must withdraw and resubmit.

**Q: What happens if my subscription expires?**
A: You cannot submit new bids, but existing bids and contracts remain valid.

**Q: How do I request a debriefing after evaluation?**
A: Contact NPC within 14 days of receiving the evaluation result.
