# PNG National e-Government Procurement System
## Phased Implementation Plan & Payment Milestones
### Document Version: 1.0 | Date: January 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the phased implementation approach for the PNG National e-Government Procurement System, with detailed payment milestones linked to deliverable acceptance. The payment structure is designed to:

- Ensure value-for-money through milestone-based payments
- Reduce implementation risk through phased delivery
- Align contractor incentives with project success
- Maintain cash flow for implementation partners

**Total Contract Value:** USD $2,990,000
**Implementation Period:** 24 months
**Payment Terms:** Milestone-based (deliverable acceptance)

## 7. Payment Variations & Claims

### 7.1 Change Order Process

| Change Category | Approval Authority | Payment Impact |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Minor (< $10,000) | Project Manager | Within contingency |
| Medium ($10,000 - $50,000) | Project Director | Steering Committee notification |
| Major (> $50,000) | Steering Committee | Contract amendment required |

### 7.2 Delay Penalties

| Delay Duration | Penalty |
|----------------|---------|
| 1-2 weeks | Warning letter |
| 2-4 weeks | 0.5% of milestone value per week |
| 4-8 weeks | 1% of milestone value per week |
| > 8 weeks | Contract termination consideration |
| **Maximum Penalty** | 10% of contract value |

### 7.3 Early Completion Bonus

| Early Completion | Bonus |
|------------------|-------|
| 1-2 weeks early | 0.25% of milestone value |
| 2-4 weeks early | 0.5% of milestone value |
| > 4 weeks early | 1% of milestone value |
| **Maximum Bonus** | 5% of contract value |
